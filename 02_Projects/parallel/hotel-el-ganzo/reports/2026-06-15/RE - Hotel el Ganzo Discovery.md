### Budget Structure (Alvaro's Formula)

- Total budget: $60,000-$80,000 USD
- 35% equipment (~$24,500 at midpoint) — Parallel purchases all gear, +15% fee on materials
- 35% salaries (~$24,500 at midpoint) — roles disclosed to client, amounts internal
  - Luca salary (65%): ~$13,000
  - Partner salary (35%): ~$7,000
  - Buffer for overruns: ~$4,500
- 30% gross profit (~$21,000 at midpoint)
  - Consultant fee: -$5,000 (technical plan development, from profit)
  - Net profit: ~$16,000
    - 50% retained by business (~$8,000) — operating reserve, gear, marketing
    - 50% distributed as owners' draw (50/50 equal split): ~$4,000 each

**Total per person at $70K midpoint:**
- Luca: $13,000 salary + $4,000 draw = $17,000
- Partner: $7,000 salary + $4,000 draw = $11,000
