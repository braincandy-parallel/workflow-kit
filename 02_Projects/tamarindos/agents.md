# Tamarindos — Agent Context

Read root `CLAUDE.md` first.

## Project Overview

Tamarindos is a Mexican farm-to-table restaurant in Los Cabos, owned by Luca's father. The business is currently going through a serious decline. Luca serves multiple roles: marketing, IT, lighting, and managing 3 Airbnb listings tied to the property. Luca is exploring how to use Claude to help stabilize and grow the business.

## Active Areas

- Marketing (social, content, campaigns)
- IT systems
- Lighting for the venue
- Airbnb management (3 listings)
- Business strategy and recovery
