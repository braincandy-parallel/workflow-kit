## Video Production

### Paella Saturday (Jun 2026) — post-review notes

**Lens cleanliness:** Check and wipe the lens before every shoot. Some clips in the Paella video had softness/haze from a dirty lens that was only visible in the final edit.

**Exposure in mixed light:** The restaurant has areas with strong sunlight and deep shadow. Clips shot across that contrast came out washed out. Prioritize exposing for the subject — adjust angle or timing to avoid shooting directly into bright windows. Review clips on the spot before moving on.

**Showing restaurant scale:** GM requested zoom-ins, but digital zoom loses quality. Better alternative: gimbal walkthroughs that move through the space so viewers feel the size of the restaurant. Plan these as dedicated shots, not incidental movement.

**Clip length vs. coverage:** Individual clips at 2-3 seconds don't leave room for walkthrough or environmental storytelling. On future shoots, capture longer continuous takes (8-15 sec) even if only 2-3 seconds end up in the edit — gives more flexibility in post.
